# FAQ — Existing Clients

## I'm already working with Zartis — who do I contact for questions about my engagement?

Your account manager is the right contact for anything related to an active engagement — team changes, billing, contract questions, or anything operational. If you're not sure who your account manager is, reach out to the main contact email and it will be routed correctly.

## I have a technical question about my current project — who handles that?

Technical questions about an active project should go directly to the lead engineer on your account, or to your account manager who can route it. The chat on this website is for new enquiries, not for support on existing engagements.

## I want to expand the scope of our current engagement — who should I talk to?

Your account manager. They have the context on your current engagement and can assess whether the expansion fits within the existing structure or requires a new scoping conversation.

## I want to give feedback about the team or the work — how?

Directly to your account manager. Zartis takes feedback seriously — it's how engagements get better and how the team learns. There's no formal ticketing system for feedback on active accounts; a direct conversation is faster and more effective.

## I'm an existing client and I want to explore a new AI initiative separate from my current engagement — is that the right conversation for here?

Yes — if it's a genuinely new problem or initiative, the chat is a reasonable starting point. Mention that you're an existing client; it'll help the conversation move faster.
