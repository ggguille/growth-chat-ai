# AI Development

Build intelligent systems, automate workflows, and deliver AI-driven products that scale.

## What This Covers

Zartis builds and integrates high-impact AI features, from automated processes to LLM-powered capabilities, delivering the technical backbone for safe and reliable operation.

### Agentic AI Development

We design and build agentic architectures where multiple agents plan, reason, and collaborate across complex workflows. We develop the underlying logic, communication, and safety layers to run these powerful systems reliably in real-world environments.

### GenAI Development

We engineer GenAI capabilities that slot cleanly into existing products and operations. The focus is on turning real workflows into reliable systems, with the right data, integrations, and quality controls to make solutions secure, measurable, and scalable. This includes LLM application development and RAG (retrieval-augmented generation) pipelines.

### Semantic Layer and Data Platform Development

We build the data foundation AI needs to work. This includes designing AI-specific data platforms, engineering the pipelines that feed models, and ensuring data is production-ready. Not data infrastructure for its own sake — data infrastructure that makes AI actually work.

### AI Architecture

We design the technical infrastructure that runs AI in production: cloud architecture, model deployment and optimisation, integration with existing systems, and the operational framework that keeps AI running reliably. Production-ready architecture, not proof-of-concept setups.

### AI Implementation

We build and deploy AI capabilities end-to-end — architecture, engineering, testing, and production rollout — complemented by the guardrails, monitoring, and iteration needed to perform reliably over time.

### AIOps and Infrastructure

We move organisations beyond proof of concept to enterprise-grade AI operations, providing the specialised infrastructure and operational expertise required for mission-critical AI systems.

## Who This Is For

Companies with a clear AI use case that need the engineering depth to build it to production quality. Typically: engineering teams that want to add AI capabilities to existing products; companies building new AI-first products; organisations moving past a prototype phase to production.

Zartis is an Anthropic partner and a Microsoft partner. We support organisations building with Claude and Claude Code, from implementation and integration through to enablement and adoption.
