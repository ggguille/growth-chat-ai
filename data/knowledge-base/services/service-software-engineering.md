# Software Engineering

Modern software development with a strong engineering culture — from strategy to implementation.

## What This Covers

Zartis provides end-to-end support across the software engineering lifecycle. Whether clients need expert guidance to refine development processes or hands-on engineering to build and deploy solutions, the scope is broad and the delivery is senior.

### Software Development

Full-stack engineering across a wide range of technologies and domains. Development teams that integrate directly into client product squads.

### Software Architecture

Architecture consulting — designing systems for scale, resilience, and long-term maintainability. Independent review and advisory for existing systems as well as greenfield design.

### Data Engineering

Building the data pipelines, environments, and operational workflows that AI and analytical systems depend on. Data that is reliable, well-structured, and production-ready.

### AI-Enabled Engineering

Helping engineering teams adopt AI tooling safely and effectively across the SDLC — improving velocity and quality without compromising engineering standards. This includes AI code review, AI-assisted testing, and Claude Code adoption.

### DevOps

Infrastructure, CI/CD pipelines, deployment workflows, and operational practices that allow teams to ship reliably and at speed.

### Quality Engineering (QA)

Test strategy, automation, and quality practices embedded across the development lifecycle — not bolted on at the end.

### SDLC Optimisation

Process improvement for engineering organisations: identifying bottlenecks, improving delivery practices, and building the engineering culture that sustains consistent high output.

### Cloud Services

Cloud migration, modernisation, cost optimisation, and cloud-native architecture. Also SecOps and AIOps.

### Specialist Advisory

M&A deal readiness, technology due diligence, post-merger integration, and complex system integrations for companies in transition.

## How Delivery Works

Zartis engineers work as part of the client's team. The default is embedded delivery — Zartis people inside the client's organisation, operating on the client's cadence, contributing to the client's product. The engagement structure (team extension, end-to-end delivery, or advisory) is chosen based on what the client actually needs.

## Scale

280+ engineers across 60+ clients. Offices and delivery hubs in Ireland, Germany, UK, Spain, Poland, Portugal, Croatia, and distributed remotely across Europe, Latin America, and beyond.
