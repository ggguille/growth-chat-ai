# Locations and Timezone Coverage

## Offices and Delivery Hubs

Zartis has offices and delivery hubs in:

- **Ireland** (Cork — headquarters)
- **Germany** (Berlin)
- **United Kingdom**
- **Spain** (Madrid)
- **Poland** (Wroclaw)
- **Portugal**
- **Croatia**

Distributed remote engineers also operate from Cyprus, South Africa, Egypt, Latin America, the US, and beyond.

## European Timezone Coverage

The majority of Zartis' engineering capacity operates in CET/GMT (Central European Time and UK time). For clients based in the US, this means meaningful daily overlap — the Zartis team is at work while US teams are finishing the previous day and starting the current one.

For UK and EU clients, the coverage is full working-day overlap with no timezone gap.

## Why Nearshore European Matters

The European nearshore model is built around cultural alignment, language compatibility, and timezone proximity. Unlike deep offshore models (India, Southeast Asia), European nearshore teams work on the same working day as most clients, attend the same meetings, and operate in the same regulatory environment.

For AI and software engineering work specifically, this matters for:
- **Daily standups and sprint ceremonies** — real-time participation, not async-only
- **Code review and pair programming** — same-day turnaround
- **Client escalations** — people available when the client needs them

## Talent Distribution

Zartis' philosophy is that talent is not limited to a specific timezone or geography. The hiring model selects on technical depth and cultural fit, not location. The distributed delivery model allows access to specialist AI and engineering talent across Europe and beyond, while maintaining the communication and collaboration quality of a local team.
