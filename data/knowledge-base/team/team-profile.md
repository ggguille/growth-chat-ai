# Team Profile

## Who We Are

Zartis is a global technology consulting partner specialising in AI transformation and advanced engineering. Founded in Cork, Ireland in 2009, we have grown to 280+ engineers working with 60+ clients across a wide range of industries and technologies.

We are an Anthropic partner and a Microsoft partner — working with the tools and platforms that matter in production AI.

## Size and Scale

280+ engineers. 60+ active clients. Engagements across Financial Services, Energy & Climate, Healthcare, Regulatory Compliance, Logistics, Telecommunications, and Education.

## Seniority

We work with senior engineers. The model is built around people who can contribute at a high technical level quickly — not large teams of generalists, but specialists who understand the domain and can work as peers with client engineering teams.

## Values

- **Integrity:** Transparency in everything. Trust is the most important asset, maintained with clients and within the team.
- **Diversity:** Different backgrounds make the team stronger. Inclusive by design.
- **Ownership:** Engineers take responsibility for outcomes, not just tasks.
- **Innovation:** The first instinct is to ask "how else." People who question, learn, and try new things.
- **Quality:** The standard is exceeding expectations. Long-term client relationships are built on consistently delivering quality work.

## How We Work

Zartis engineers work as part of the client's team. The default is embedded delivery — Zartis people operating on the client's cadence, using the client's processes, contributing to the client's product. The register is peer-to-peer: senior engineers working with senior engineers, not a vendor relationship.

## Our Story

Zartis started in Cork, Ireland in 2009 as a SaaS company in HR software. It evolved into a services company and expanded internationally — Spain (2015), Germany (2017), Poland (2017), Portugal and Czech Republic (2021). By 2023 we had added Croatia and were 280+ engineers strong. In 2025, the company completed its pivot to an AI transformation–focused consultancy, building proprietary AI capabilities and supporting clients moving beyond AI pilots to production-ready systems.
